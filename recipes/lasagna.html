Lasagna

Description

A popular italian dish made by layering pasta, sauce, and cheese, then baking it until hot and golden.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • nutmeg
  • 300g fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a pan and add the 750g lean beef mince and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the 90g chopped prosciutto.
  4. Add 800g passata and 200ml hot beef stock.
  5. Season with a pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  6. Lightly grease baking dish with olive oil.
  7. Start with a spoonful of meat sauce at the bottom.
  8. Add a layer of 300g fresh lasagne sheets.
  9. Spread some white sauce over the pasta.
  10. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, finishing with white sauce and mozzarella on top.
  11. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
